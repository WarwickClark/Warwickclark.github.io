About Me

I have always love coding since I first started in high school, but I was not sure if I wanted to proceed with coding as my career path. So I decided to go oversees and work and travel to see what I wanted to do as my career.

I had many jobs in the UK and USA spanning from house keeping to snowboard instructer.Eventually I ended up in a banquet kitchen in Florida, USA.I loved cooking professionaly, and moved around the United States cooking in kitchens and learning along the way. When I returned to South Africa, I attended Culinary school in 2010 and got my Diploma in Culinary arts.

I worked in restaurants, pubs, hotels and catering businesses. I got the opportunity to open up a new restaurant chain called the Rock Diner. We had two sites and I helped out from designing the kitchens, equipment purchase and layouts. After hiring and training staff, menu creation came and I took the photos that you see below. We opened two shops flawlessly, followed by a cocktail, tapas lounge.

I left the food industry with a heavy heart, but excited to start in the world of software Engineering. Thank you for taking the time to read my About me page, and I can't wait to hear from you.

Regards
